// Our History

Ship Dealership.

With a vision to revolutionize the maritime industry, ShipDecKK began as a small but ambitious dealership, quickly establishing itself as a leader in providing premium vessels for both leisure and commercial needs.

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK has grown into a leading ship dealership, offering a diverse range of high-quality vessels, from luxury yachts to commercial cargo ships. With a strong dedication to excellence, Integrity, Reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation as a trusted name in the maritime industry. Our journey is driven by a passion for delivering exceptional service and helping clients navigate the seas with confidence.

// Vision & Mission

Expertise That Shapes Our Vision and Mission.

We envision becoming a trusted leader in maritime solutions by delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable services. Our mission is to provide exceptional ship sale and purchase assistance, custom shipbuilding, comprehensive maintenance, insurance, and after-sales support—driven by a team of experts committed to excellence and client satisfaction.
